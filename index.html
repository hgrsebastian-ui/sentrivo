Privacy & control

Connecting Gmail requires trust. Here's exactly how it works.

Google OAuth connection

We use Google's official OAuth flow. Your password is never shared with us.

Read-only by default

Sentrivo reads your emails to organise them. It never writes or sends without your action.

Nothing is sent without your approval

Every application draft is yours to review, edit, and approve before it leaves your inbox.

Revoke access anytime

Disconnect Sentrivo from your Google account in seconds from Google's security settings.
