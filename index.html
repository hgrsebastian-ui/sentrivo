How it works

You spend thirty seconds. Sentrivo spends the rest of the week.

The parts that take hours: research, finding the right contact, writing a specific letter for each, tracking the threads, following up. Sentrivo does all of that in the background.

What you do

≈ 30 seconds

01 Upload your CV

A PDF is fine. Sentrivo parses your background, experience, and skills.

02 Write one sentence

"Summer internship in policy research, London." That's the brief.

03 Review and approve

Each draft sits in a queue for you to read. Edit anything. Hit send when ready.

What Sentrivo does

All week, in the background

04 Finds 20 best-fit organisations

Scores each match against your CV and brief. Finds the right contact email, or the most-watched general inbox if no contact is public.

05 Writes a specific cover letter for each

Not a template with the org name swapped in. Every draft references concrete details about that organisation and connects your experience to their work.

06 Follows up after two weeks

If they didn't reply, Sentrivo drafts a follow-up. If they did, it sorts the thread by what's needed next.

Privacy & control

Connecting Gmail requires trust. Here's exactly how it works.

01

Google OAuth

Sentrivo uses Google's official OAuth flow. Your password is never shared.

02

Read-only by default

We read your inbox to organise it. We never write or send without your action.

03

Nothing sends automatically

Every email is yours to review, edit, and approve before it leaves your inbox.

04

Revoke anytime

Disconnect Sentrivo in seconds from Google's security settings.
